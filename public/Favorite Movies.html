Dan's Favorite Movies

The Best Movies I've Ever Seen

The Fall

The colors, the locations, the costumes, the acting - in every way this movie was just an aboslute masterpiece of cinema that is severely underrated and always in recommendation list.

Muppet Treasure Island

The best Muppet movie they ever made. Fight me about it. Brian Henson's era of the Muppets is severly overlooked by purists.
Rizzo and Gonzo for liiiiife!!!

Promising Young Woman

I'm biased because I worked on it. But stylistically it's daring, Mulligan's acting is amazing, and the first time I read the script I was emotionally shaken by the ending.
